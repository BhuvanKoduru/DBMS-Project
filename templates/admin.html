Hello {{ n }}! Lets Get Started with Time Table Generator

Welcome to time table generator, a timetable management system for our college to schedule the department timetable. Get started by referring to the flow of the system below or Contact Us with any queries or difficulties.

Get Started »   Contact Us »

1. Add Teachers

Navigate to the "Add Teachers" section and add the list of teachers for the semester.

2. Add Rooms

Navigate to the "Add Rooms" section and add the corresponding details. Select one of the rooms from Lab rooms for Lab classes and regular rooms for theory subjects.

3. Add Timings

Navigate to the "Add Timings" section and add the timings for each day of the week.


4. Add Courses

Navigate to the "Add Courses" section and add the subject codes and subject names for the semester.

5. Add Dept(CSBS, IS)

Choose to the department for which timetable is to be generated.

6. Generate

Navigate to the "Generate Timetable" Section and click on Generate Timetable and wait patiently as our algorithm works its magic.
